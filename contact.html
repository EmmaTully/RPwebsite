Frequently Asked Questions

All our published research papers are available through our Publications page, with links to journal articles, arXiv preprints, and supplemental materials. Many papers are open access and freely available.

We welcome academic visitors and collaborators. Please contact us at research@recognitionphysics.org to arrange visits, seminars, or collaborative meetings with our research team.

Citation information is provided with each publication on our website. For the foundational framework, please cite: Washburn, J. "The Meta-Principle: A Tautological Foundation for Physics" Physical Review D (2024).

We offer research internships, postdoctoral positions, and collaborative PhD opportunities. We also provide workshops on Lean 4 verification and Recognition Physics principles. Contact us for current openings.

There are many ways to contribute: experimental validation of predictions, formal verification of proofs, applications development, or theoretical extensions. Contact our research team to discuss how your expertise might contribute.
