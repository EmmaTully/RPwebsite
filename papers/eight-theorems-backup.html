Summary

This paper systematically derives Recognition Science's complete mathematical foundation through eight forced theorems (T1-T8). Starting from the Meta-Principle tautology "nothing cannot recognize itself," it proves atomic tick constraints, discrete continuity, potential uniqueness, the forced cost function J(x)=½(x+x⁻¹)-1, eight-tick scheduling minimality, coverage bounds, and integer δ-units. Abstract

We show that a single logical tautology—the Meta-Principle (MP), "nothing cannot recognize itself"—forces eight core theorems (T1–T8) that pin down the recognition ledger, the unique convex symmetric cost J(x)=½(x+x⁻¹)-1 (with fixed local scale), the golden ratio fixed point φ via φ²=φ+1, an eight-tick minimal update cycle (2³), coverage lower bounds, and integer δ-units (ℤ). Formalized in Lean 4, these results constitute a machine-verifiable spine which, combined with bridge factorization through units and the exclusivity/inevitability certificates, yields a parameter-free derivation chain MP → φ → (α, C_lag) → gravity w(r) with zero tunable constants.

  • T1. Meta-Principle: Logical tautology: ¬∃r∈Recognition(∅,∅)
  • T2. Atomic Tick: Exactly one posting per tick (no concurrency)
  • T3. Discrete Continuity: Closed-chain flux is zero
  • T4. Potential Uniqueness: Potentials unique up to additive constant on components
  • T5. Cost Uniqueness: J(x)=½(x+x⁻¹)-1 uniquely under stated hypotheses
  • T6. Eight-Tick Minimality: T_min=2³; for D=3, T=8 exists and is minimal
  • T7. Coverage Bound: T<2³ cannot cover all classes
  • T8. δ-Units: Ledger increments ≅ℤ via n↦n·δ

Implications

This work completes the logical foundation of Recognition Science by showing that all core mathematical structure is forced by a single tautology. The resulting parameter-free framework makes rigid predictions that are either right or wrong—no adjustable parameters to tune. The eight-theorem spine provides the mathematical backbone for parameter-free derivation of physical constants (α, fine structure), galaxy rotation curves without dark matter, quantum mechanics from recognition ledger dynamics, and inevitable emergence of space-time structure.

Under completeness and absence of external scale, Recognition Science becomes the unique fundamental framework—not one model among many, but the necessary structure reality must have. The framework shifts the burden of falsifiability from foundational axioms to the deductive chain itself, creating a theory that is immune to overthrow at its foundations while remaining fully testable through its necessary consequences.
